Saharan Symphony Supergrains and Spices Bowl

Description

Take a culinary journey to Africa with this flavorful and aromatic rice bowl. Nutty basmati rice is paired with a spice-infused mix of chickpeas, sweet potatoes, and kale. The vegetables and legumes are seasoned with a blend of curry powder, turmeric, and cumin, providing a warming and earthy flavor profile. Topped with fresh cilantro, this bowl offers a taste of African cuisine that's as nutritious as it is delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of basmati rice
  • 1 can of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 large sweet potato, cubed
  • 2 cups of kale, chopped
  • 1 large red onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the basmati rice according to the instructions on the package.
  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sweet potato cubes and cook until they're soft and browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Cook until they're soft and fragrant.
  4. Add the chopped kale to the pan and stir to combine. Cook until the kale is wilted.
  5. Add the curry powder, turmeric, cumin, salt, and pepper to the pan. Stir to combine.
  6. Add the chickpeas and cooked sweet potato back to the pan and stir to combine with the spices and kale.
  7. Divide the cooked rice into bowls, top with the chickpea and vegetable mixture, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve.
